"Huh?"

Mechatronics is a new field of engineering that not many people know of. It is a combination of electrical and mechanical engineering, hence the name (mecha-mechanical, tronics – electronics). The focus is on the integration of mechanical and electronic systems. It covers a wide range of subjects from both mechanical and electrical engineering degrees. Where this field is mainly applicable is in robotics, computer systems and automation. Because core fundamental knowledge is attained from both fields, a mechatronics engineer can expand into either field, allowing for a highly flexible engineer.
